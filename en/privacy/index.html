Data protection

Privacy & cookie policy

This notice describes how the website operates and how personal data is handled when you contact TSANTOS.

Data controller

The website is operated by the TSANTOS business at Stoupa, Lefktro 240 24. For personal-data enquiries, contact info@tsantosconstruction.gr or 27210 64040.

Data that may be processed

The website has no contact form, user accounts, or online purchasing. When you contact us directly by phone or email, we process the details and content you choose to provide so that we can respond.

The hosting provider may record technical access data such as IP address, date, time, requested page, and browser information for security, operation, and troubleshooting.

Purposes and legal bases

Direct contact details are used to respond to enquiries, provide product information, and discuss delivery. Processing is based on handling your request, pre-contractual steps where applicable, legal obligations, and the legitimate interest in operating a secure website.

Recipients and transfers

Access is limited to people who need the information to handle the enquiry and technical providers supporting website hosting and operation. We do not sell personal data.

The contact page embeds a Google map. When the map loads, Google may receive technical data such as the IP address and browser information. Google’s terms and privacy policy apply.

Retention

We retain correspondence only as long as needed to respond to and manage the related enquiry and for any period required by lawful tax, accounting, or other obligations. Technical logs are retained according to the hosting provider’s security configuration.

Cookies and analytics

The website does not use its own optional cookies, audience analytics, or advertising pixels. The contact page includes an embedded Google map, which is subject to Google’s privacy and cookie practices.

If a consent-requiring service is added later, this policy will be updated and the service will remain disabled until the visitor makes a valid choice.

Your rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may request information, access, correction, erasure, restriction, or objection to processing and withdraw any consent you have given.

You may also complain to the Hellenic Data Protection Authority if you believe processing infringes applicable data-protection law.

Policy updates

This policy is updated when website behavior or relevant legal obligations change. The latest revision date appears at the end of this page.
